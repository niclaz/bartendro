## Drinks Can't be Made Screen

If you get this page with a sick looking robot on it. Fear not, the mend is just around the bend.

### Step 1 — Go to the Admin Screen
- You get a sick looking Bartendro if it can't make any drinks, and that usually stems from two possibilities:
  - You only have one Dispenser connected.
  - You don't have a combination of ingredients in the Admin > Dispensers page that make a proper cocktail.
- Tap or click the Bartendro in the top right corner to get to the Admin screen to make changes to your Dispenser ingredient lineup.
- You may be prompted for a password, if so: bartendro / boozemeup

### Step 2 — Select Good Ingredient Combinations
- Either add more Dispensers, or select ingredients that combine to make a drink.
- When you select a Dispenser's ingredient, you will get a drop down menu to select another ingredient, all of which come from Admin > booze.
- When you select all your desired ingredients and hit save at the bottom of the screen, you will see the area above the dispensers (highlighted in red) get updated with the number of drinks that can be made with that combination of ingredients.

### Step 3 — Dispense Shots
- If you only have one Dispenser, then you can't really be making cocktails. However, you can go to the Shots tab and dispense shots of the ingredient that you have.
- You can adjust the size of shots under Admin > options.
